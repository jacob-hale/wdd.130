Colorado River

Info about the river: Begins in Northern Colorado and ends in the Gulf of California, 1,450 miles, This river passes through 6 different states and 11 different national parks. Info about trip: Intermediate difficulty, Total time on water: about 90 minutes, Sites visited: "Davey Jone's Locker", "Palace of the gods", "Serpent's Back"

Salmon River

Info about the river: Central Idaho, 425 miles, for approximately 180 miles, the Salmon Canyon is more than one mile deep. Info about the trip: Beginner difficulty, Total time on water: about 60 minutes, Sites visited: "Beans Arch", "The Mile Deep", Posiedon's Toilet"

Snake River

Info about the river: Begins in Western Wyoming and ends at the Colombia River, 1,078 miles, the largest North American river to empty into the Pacific Ocean. Info about the trip: Expert difficulty, Total time on water: 100 minutes, Sites visited: "The Potatoe Rock", "Idaho Falls Temple", "The Birth Canal"
